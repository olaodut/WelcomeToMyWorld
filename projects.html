How It Works

Here’s how our free cybersecurity process works:

  1. Initial Contact: Reach out to us through our website or via email, and fill out a simple intake form with your business details.
  2. Vulnerability and Network Scanning: We use tools to assess your network for vulnerabilities, open ports, and misconfigurations.
  3. Report Generation: We compile our findings into a detailed PDF report, which includes security recommendations tailored to your business.
  4. Review and Recommendations: We walk you through the results and explain how to fix the issues we've identified.
  5. Follow-Up: We offer free follow-up s after you've implemented our recommendations to ensure your security is up to standard.
